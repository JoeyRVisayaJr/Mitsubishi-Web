MITSUBISHI

FINANCING OPTIONS
This page provides information regarding the requirements to avail Mitsubishi Motors' cars based on income categories and the accredited bank partners

Employee Income

Requirements:

COE or last 3 months payslips (with salary, allowance or commission)

Valid IDs (ex. TIN ID), Company ID, Proof of Billing

Co-borrower requirement: 2 Valid IDs

Government Employee Income

Requirements:

COE or last 3 months payslips (with salary, allowance or commission)

VALID IDs (ex. TIN ID), Government Employee ID, and Proof of Billing

Co-borrower requirement: 2 Valid IDs

Seaman

Requirements:

Seaman Booklet, Passport ID, and Latest Contract

Proof of Remittance or Allotment

Primary Borrower (Wife or Parents) Requirements: 2 Valid IDs and Passbook

Overseas Filipino Worker (OFW)

Requirements:

2 Valid IDs, Proof of Billing, Contract, and POEA

Bank Statement

Primary Borrower Requirements: 2 Valid IDs, Proof of Remittance, Passbook

Business Income

Requirements:

Bank Statement (Passbook or last 3 months record)

DTI Permit or Mayor's Permit, Valid IDs (ex. TIN ID), Driver License, Proof of Billing, and ITR with AFS

Co-borrower requirements: 2 Valid IDs
